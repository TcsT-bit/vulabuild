Adopt VulaBuild for Transparent Public Projects

The Problem
SA loses R300B+ yearly to tender corruption. Projects stall, youth stay jobless.

The Solution
VulaBuild splits contracts into community-managed projects. Leaders vote on suppliers, payments only release on verified work, everything is logged publicly.

Why It Works
- Kills corruption with transparent logs
- Creates jobs: 1 contract = 20 micro-businesses
- Legal under Public Procurement Act 2024
- Works offline with USSD/SMS backup
